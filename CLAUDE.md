# Gloss Specification Guidelines

## Jekyll
- Entire spec wrapped in single `{% raw %}` / `{% endraw %}` block (no individual blocks)

## Formatting
- Label separator placeholder: `␠|␠` (no extra spaces inside backticks)
- Cross-references: "(see § X)" format

## Editorial
- **Single source of truth**: Define terms once, reference elsewhere
- **Terminology**: Use defined terms consistently ("Content" not "narrative text", "Codex Traits" not just "Traits")
- **Active voice**: "that Codex preserves" not "preserved by Codex"
- **Commas**: After introductory words ("Otherwise, ...")

## Structure
- Avoid orphaned sentences after lists — integrate or move before
- Avoid single-item bullet lists — convert to prose
- Consolidate consecutive Notes when covering related topics
- Scope-setting statements belong in section intros, not buried in subsections
